Aufnahmeprüfung Preparation for Studienkolleg

Most of the students waste on average 1 - 2 semesters to get a place in Studienkolleg. A high competition and tough exam model makes the entrance exam more complex. Many students end up joining expensive private Studienkolleg in Germany. Thus, this couse is designed to prepare the students appearing Studienkolleg.

The entrance exam - Aufnahmeprüfung

The "Aufnahmeprüfung" for Studienkolleg is a pivotal assessment for international students seeking entry into German universities for Bacherlor. It takes place once in a semester (or year). This comprehensive entrance exam meticulously evaluates language proficiency and subject-specific knowledge tailored to the chosen academic track (M-Kurs, T-Kurs G/S-Kurs und W-Kurs). Subjects encompass core disciplines such as mathematics, physics, chemistry, and language skills, aligning with the respective course of study. Success in this exam is paramount, serving as the key determinant for admission to Studienkolleg programs. It ensures that students possess the necessary foundation to navigate the academic demands of higher education in Germany. Thorough preparation for the diverse and challenging questions is advised, underscoring the significance of this examination in shaping students' academic trajectories.

We have designed a special course for students appearing Aufnahmeprüfung for Studienkolleg. This 6-weeks course is designed in such a way that, it covers all necessary information regarding Aufnahmeprüfung taking place at different universities in Germany. This course includes the preparation of C-Test, Mathematics, Natural Sciences, Letter writing,etc., so that students do not have to appear for a second attempt.
