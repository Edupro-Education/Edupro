German Language

A1

This basic level covers learning the fundamentals of German grammar as well as basic vocabulary including alphabet, pronunciation, and introductions. By this level, you are able to perform short-talks and comprehend short sentences.

German Language

B1

The B1 level represents an intermediate language proficiency where learners can engage in routine conversations and express opinions. At this stage, individuals can understand and respond to familiar topics, navigate daily situations, and grasp the main points of texts. B1 signifies a practical grasp of the German language for practical communication.

Studienkolleg

Aufnahmeprüfung Preparation for Studienkolleg

Most student waste on average 1-2 semesters to get a place for Studienkolleg in Germany. To address this problem, we have designed a special course for students appearing Aufnahmeprüfung for Studienkolleg. This 6-weeks course is designed in such a way that, it covers all necessary information regarding Aufnahmeprüfung taking place at different universities in Germany. This course includes the preparation of C-Test, Mathematics, Natural Sciences, Letter writing,etc., so that students do not have to appear for a second attempt.
